Projects

Ambulance Request Automated System

An automated web-based ambulance request system that allows patients to request emergency assistance online, automatically prioritizes the emergency (green, orange, or red), and assigns the correct ambulance type (BLS, ILS, or ALS) based on severity.

The system prevents requests when no ambulances are available and supports three user roles: patients, paramedics, and managers. Patients can make requests and update payments, paramedics can update service details and treatment provided, and managers can monitor all daily operations, including completed and pending emergencies, paramedic assignments, ambulance availability, and payment records.

Back to Earth Quiz

Built and programmed an interactive quiz using C++ and Qt. The quiz follows the story of an astronaut who is lost in space and must answer questions correctly to get back to Earth before time runs out (#Energy).

The quiz allows users to create a username the first time they play. The game has three levels, ranging from easy to hard, and the player must pass all levels to reach Earth.

At the end, players can view the leaderboard to see their ranking compared to all other players who have played the game.
